Orientation

OSP models an operational system as a dependency graph, places it in geographic and temporal context, and computes what breaks, where, and when. One selection is shared across the Map, Graph, and Risk views; the timeline scrubber changes what exists — and what is critical — at the current hour. All data is local; the app makes no network calls.

Honesty notes

Criticality is a structural estimate from topology and analyst-entered importance values — it is not a simulation and predicts no outcomes. Auto-detected findings are rule-based and require analyst review; they render distinct from analyst-asserted vulnerabilities. The built-in dataset is fictional and unclassified.
